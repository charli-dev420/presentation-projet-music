## About

DAWWW-CORE and UnicorSoundEngine are part of the same music-tech portfolio, but they are separate project lines.

DAWWW-CORE is the browser DAW / local-first music workspace.

UnicorSoundEngine is the separate VST / plugin ecosystem.

DAWWW-CORE does not load native VST plugins in the browser.
