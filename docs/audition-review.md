# Audition & Review

Audio products need more than code.

The audition and review layer exists to structure listening notes, preset checks, visual asset review, verdicts and release-readiness decisions.

---

## Role

This layer supports Unicorn Sound Engine and the broader music-tech work by turning subjective review into something easier to track:

- what was listened to;
- what was accepted;
- what needs review;
- what should be refused;
- which presets or assets need correction;
- what can be exported as structured feedback.

---

## Review targets

The review workflow can cover:

- synth presets;
- FX presets;
- audio examples;
- manuals and product claims;
- visual assets;
- cards, icons, backgrounds and texture-like material;
- product pages and release material.

---

## Why it matters

A plugin line can look finished while still being weak in listening quality, preset clarity, documentation or packaging.

The review layer is here to avoid that. It gives the product line a way to separate:

```text
usable → needs review → refused → banned / not for release
```

---

## Français

L’audition et la revue servent à structurer les écoutes, les notes, les verdicts, les presets, les assets visuels et les décisions de release.

Le but est simple : éviter que les choix restent dans la mémoire ou dans des dossiers dispersés. Chaque élément doit pouvoir être accepté, revu, refusé ou exclu avec une raison claire.
