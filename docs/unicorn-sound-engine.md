# Unicorn Sound Engine

**Unicorn Sound Engine** is the separate plugin ecosystem around the music-tech work.

It belongs to the same portfolio as DAWWW-CORE, but it is not the DAWWW-CORE runtime. DAWWW-CORE is a browser DAW; Unicorn Sound Engine is the VST/plugin product line.

---

## Product role

Unicorn Sound Engine gathers the material needed to make audio plugins understandable, testable and distributable:

- VST instruments;
- VST effects;
- manuals and user-facing documentation;
- listening review notes;
- audition material;
- packaging and installer work;
- store and download flows;
- release and version notes.

---

## Relationship with DAWWW-CORE

DAWWW-CORE and Unicorn Sound Engine share the same music-tech direction, but they stay separate:

| DAWWW-CORE | Unicorn Sound Engine |
| --- | --- |
| Browser DAW | VST/plugin ecosystem |
| Web Audio runtime | Native plugin distribution |
| `.dw` project continuity | Instruments, FX, manuals and downloads |
| Desktop / Android testing | Packaging, store, listening review |

This separation matters because it keeps the product story honest and technically clear.

---

## What this line should prove

- The plugins have a clear role.
- The manuals and pages explain what each product is for.
- The downloads and packaging path can be trusted.
- Listening review is treated as product work, not as an afterthought.
- The FX and synth families are organized as a coherent audio product ecosystem.

## Français

**Unicorn Sound Engine** est une ligne séparée autour des instruments et effets VST.

Elle complète l’univers music-tech, mais elle ne remplace pas DAWWW-CORE et ne fonctionne pas comme runtime du DAW navigateur.

Son rôle est de regrouper les plugins, manuels, notes d’écoute, audition, packaging, store, téléchargements et éléments de release autour d’une famille audio lisible.
