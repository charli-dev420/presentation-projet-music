DAW Core needs people who can help test and sharpen the browser DAW path:

- Product feedback on create/save/reopen/continue workflows.

- UX review around first session, recovery, project state, and confidence.

Unicor SoundEngine needs feedback that treats the sound line as its own product family:

- Synthé listening review by instrument.
- FX prioritization by musical value.
- Preset, naming, manual, and first-session feedback.
- VST catalog and packaging review.
- Visual and product-page polish.
