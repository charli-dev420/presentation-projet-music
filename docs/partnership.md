# Partnership Brief / Fiche partenariat

## Francais

### Besoin

DAW Core et Unicor SoundEngine cherchent a transformer un ensemble audio deja structure en produit plus stable, plus lisible et plus diffusable.

### Apports recherches

- Expertise audio produit.
- Testeurs et musiciens.
- Design sonore.
- Accompagnement distribution VST.
- Financement pour QA, packaging, UX et release.
- Opportunites emploi ou mission autour audio, produit et IA appliquee.

### Ce qui est partage publiquement

Uniquement une presentation. Les details source, assets internes, releases et preuves QA restent dans les espaces prives.

## English

### Need

DAW Core and Unicor SoundEngine aim to turn an already structured audio ecosystem into a more stable, legible, and distributable product.

### Looking for

- Audio product expertise.
- Testers and musicians.
- Sound design.
- VST distribution support.
- Funding for QA, packaging, UX, and release work.
- Employment or freelance opportunities around audio, product, and applied AI.

### What is shared publicly

Only a presentation. Source details, internal assets, releases, and QA evidence stay private.
